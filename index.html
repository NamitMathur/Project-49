Facts about camels

  • Camels have furry ears

Origami Chameleon

Facts about chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Chameleons use camoflauge to hide themselves from predators or prey
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail to wrap around tree branches

Facts about pandas
